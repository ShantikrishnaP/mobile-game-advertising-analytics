## Overview

This research project analyzed the mobile game advertising ecosystem, focusing on monetization strategies, ad mechanics, and industry measurement standards.

The work explored how brands leverage mobile in-game advertising to drive user acquisition, engagement, and revenue generation.

## Areas Covered

### 1. Mobile Advertising Ecosystem
- Display Ads
- Search Ads
- Social Ads
- Video Ads
- Native Ads

### 2. In-Game Advertising Models
- Around-game ads
- Advergames
- Static vs Dynamic in-game ads
- Interstitial ads
- Playable ads
- Rewarded ads
- Offerwall ads

### 3. Ad Experience & Game Integration
- Core loop integration strategies
- Voluntary vs involuntary ad exposure
- User targeting approaches
- SDK-based ad serving systems

### 4. Measurement & Industry Standards
- Impression counting models
- Click tracking vs video tracking
- MRC measurement guidelines
- Rewarded ad disclosure standards
